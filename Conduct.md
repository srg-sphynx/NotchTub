# Community Participation Agreement

## Our Commitment

We are dedicated to providing a safe and welcoming environment for every individual who joins our project. We commit to ensuring that participation is free from intimidation or bias. This applies regardless of personal background, physical ability, appearance, identity, or professional standing. Our goal is to maintain a space that prioritizes mutual respect and collective well-being.

---

## Guidelines for Interaction

To maintain a healthy atmosphere, we encourage the following practices:

* **Treating all colleagues** with genuine consideration and patience.
* **Valuing the diverse perspectives** and life experiences others bring to the table.
* **Offering feedback** that is helpful and receiving critiques with an open mind.
* **Owning up to errors** by offering sincere apologies and adjusting future behavior.
* **Prioritizing the long-term health** of the project over individual interests.

### Prohibited Behavior

The following actions are strictly prohibited:

* Using suggestive language or sharing inappropriate media.
* Engaging in personal insults, mockery, or inflammatory rhetoric.
* Harassing individuals through any channel of communication.
* Violating privacy by sharing contact details or sensitive data without consent.
* Any other actions that undermine a professional and cooperative work environment.

---

## Accountability

If you witness or experience behavior that violates these principles, please contact the project organizers. We will review every report with impartiality and take the necessary steps to resolve the issue.
