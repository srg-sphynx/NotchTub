# NotchTub User Manual

**Version 1.2**

## Welcome to NotchTub

NotchTub transforms your MacBook's notch into a powerful command surface for media, system monitoring, and productivity tools. Built with native SwiftUI, it integrates seamlessly with macOS while staying out of your way until needed.

**Designed with AntiGravity by VibeCoding**

---

## Installation

1.  **Drag to Applications**: Move `NotchTub.app` to your `/Applications` folder.
2.  **Launch**: Open NotchTub from your Applications folder or Launchpad.
3.  **Permissions**:
    *   **Accessibility**: Required to interact with the system UI and detect the notch area.
    *   **Screen Recording**: *Optional*, but recommended for "expanded drag detection" and cleaner visual integration.
    *   **Automation**: Required for controlling Music/Spotify.

## Settings & Customization

Access Settings by clicking the **gear icon** in the expanded notch view or via the menu bar icon.

-   **General**: Configure launch at login and menu bar icon visibility.
-   **Appearance**: Choose between "Standard" (Dynamic Island style) and "Minimal" modes. Customize width and animations.
-   **Gestures**: Enable/disable swipe gestures for media control and notch expansion.
-   **Updates**: Check for new versions of NotchTub.

---

## Troubleshooting

-   **Notch not appearing?** Ensure the app is running (check Activity Monitor or menu bar). Try toggling "Hide Menu Bar Icon" in settings if hidden.
-   **Media controls not working?** Verify "Automation" permissions in System Settings -> Privacy & Security -> Automation.
-   **Stats missing?** Some stats require a moment to initialize. Ensure no other monitoring tools are blocking access.
